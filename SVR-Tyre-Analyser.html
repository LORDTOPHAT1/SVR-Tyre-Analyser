Multi-Stint Analyser

Upload a screenshot per stint to get cross-compound wear data, pit window analysis, and Discord-ready reports.

Taking screenshots

Two methods work — use whichever suits you. Both require the tyre wear bars to be visible in the HUD at the bottom of the screen.

  1. Flat screen HUD (recommended): While driving, take a PS5 screenshot as you cross the start/finish line. The lap counter on screen is your laps completed for that stint.
  2. Replay method: After your race, open the GT7 replay. For each stint except the last, scrub to the moment your car enters the pit lane. For the final stint, scrub to the finish line crossing.

Setting pit laps

Enter the lap you pitted on for each stint. The tool calculates stint length automatically. For the last stint, enter the final race lap.

Cliff threshold

Set a cliff per stint since different compounds behave differently. The cliff is the wear % where your pace starts to fall away. The optimiser uses these to flag whether you had life left or were past cliff at each stop.
